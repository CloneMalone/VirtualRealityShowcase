Optics & Resolution

With the Meta Quest 3, virtual objects are displayed in high-definition, bringing sharper and clearer visuals to your surroundings. The enhanced optics make immersive experiences feel more lifelike with vibrant colors and crisp details.

CPU Processor

Meta Quest 3 uses the Snapdragon XR2 Gen 2 chip for super fast load times, smooth performance, and sharp graphics. Whether you're making your room into a home theater with a huge HD screen or diving into detailed, lifelike environments, everything feels better.

Controllers

Feel more in the moment with the ergonomic Touch Plus controllers, giving you realistic sensations and precise control—whether you're holding a bow, climbing skyscrapers, or flying through space. You can also just use your hands to navigate, swipe through content, or type on a virtual keyboard.
